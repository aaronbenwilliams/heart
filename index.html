HEART – proud winner of the 2014 RICS Community Benefit award for Yorkshire – is a lively enterprise, arts and community centre located right in the centre of Headingley in Leeds. A community enterprise established by Headingley Development Trust, HEART is owned and run by local residents.

At the heart of Headingley, we offer a versatile venue to hire, a spacious shared workspace, a licensed café and a varied programme of events and classes. HEART is a fully accessible venue with step free access and a lift to upper floors.
